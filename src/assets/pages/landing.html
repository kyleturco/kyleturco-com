Kyle Turco

Web | Illustration | Music

Nashville-based Designer & Developer

Kyle Turco is a web developer, designer/artist, and musician residing in Nashville, TN.

He currently works as a Software Engineer Associate at TechnologyAdvice, where he builds new business applications and maintain web properties. His skills also include user interface design, user experience optimization, multivariate testing, as well as graphic design and illustration.

As a musician, Kyle is a graduate of Belmont University with a degree in Commercial Music and a focus in Music Technology. He maintains a regular performance schedule at venues throughout Nashville. Additionally, his composition and production work has been featured in over two dozen television and internet advertising campaigns.
