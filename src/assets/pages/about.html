Nashville-based Artist & Developer

Kyle Turco is an artist, designer, software engineer, and musician residing in Nashville, TN.

He currently works as a Software Engineer at TechnologyAdvice, where he builds new business applications and maintain web properties. His skills also include user interface design, user experience optimization, and multivariate testing.

Kyle is an avid hobbyist painter and illustrator. His primary interests are oil painting and ink drawing. His work centers around everyday subject matter like architecture and natural landscapes. Mainly working for the love of creation, Kyle posts his work to Instagram and other web-based communities.

As a musician, Kyle is a graduate of Belmont University with a degree in Commercial Music and a focus in Music Technology. He maintains a regular performance schedule at venues throughout Nashville. Additionally, his composition and production work has been featured in over two dozen television and internet advertising campaigns.
